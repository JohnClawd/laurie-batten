About

I’m a marketing and communications leader based in Nova Scotia — and a former journalist who’s always been drawn to strong stories and real-world impact. I love taking big ideas and making them feel clear, human, and actionable — the kind of communications that builds trust and moves people.

In my professional role, I lead marketing and communications with the Ocean Startup Project, where I help amplify founders and programs advancing ocean innovation across Canada. Outside of work, I’m the founder of Safer Steps Chebucto, a grassroots initiative advocating for safer, more walkable and connected coastal communities.

I’m at my best when strategy meets story: shaping a message, building momentum, and turning “we should do something about this” into “here’s what we’re doing — and how you can be part of it.” You’ll also find me out running the Loop whenever I can.
