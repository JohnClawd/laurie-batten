About

I build trust with words (and sometimes with colourful chaos). I’m a marketing + communications leader in Nova Scotia, with a journalism background and a soft spot for stories that actually do something in the real world.

These days I lead marketing and comms at Ocean Startup Project, helping shine a light on founders and programs pushing ocean innovation forward across Canada. I also founded Safer Steps Chebucto — a community-led push for safer, more walkable, better-connected coastal communities.

My sweet spot is where strategy meets storytelling: make the message make sense, make it human, and then build enough momentum that people go from “someone should…” to “here’s what we’re doing.” Also: if there’s a chance to run the Loop, I’m probably taking it.
